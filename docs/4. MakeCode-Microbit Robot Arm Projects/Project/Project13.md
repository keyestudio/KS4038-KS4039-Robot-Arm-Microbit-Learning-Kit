# **Project 13 Rotation of Robot Arm and Control Pins**

## Part 1：Rotation of Robot Arm and Servo Angle Setting

| Servo                              | 0°                                                          | 180°                                                       |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Servo 1（Right Servo）**         | The small pendulum connected to servo 1 swings **backward** | The small pendulum connected to servo 1 swings **forward** |
| **Servo 2（Base Rotation Servo）** | Turn to the **far right**                                   | Turn to the **far left**                                   |
| **Servo 3（Claw Servo）**          | The claw will **open**                                      | The claw will **close**                                    |
| **Servo 4（Left Servo）**          | The big pendulum swings **forward**                         | The big pendulum swings **backward**                       |
